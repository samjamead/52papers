Contribute to 52 Papers

If you'd like to summarise a research article for 52 Papers, your contribution will be greatfully received.

Here's the preferred process:

  • Read a paper.
  • Summarise the paper, leaning on three or four key subheadings to present your summary.
  • Share your summary in Google Docs format with samjamead+52papers@gmail.com.
  • We'll review it and find a slot in the release queue.

We look forward to hearing from you.
